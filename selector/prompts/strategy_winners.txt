STRATEGY LENS: Positional momentum + strength (CANSLIM / Livermore style).
You are looking for market-leading stocks with strong relative strength.

PRIMARY SIGNALS (already verified for funnel survivors — weight heavily):
- Price above 50 & 200 DMA
- Near 52-week high (within ~15%)
- Beating Nifty over ~6 months

SECONDARY SIGNALS (judge from dossier):
- ROE, reasonable P/E, tight base, volume on breakout (chart_shape + volume_vs_20d_avg)

MARKET REGIME:
- Nifty above 200 DMA: standard bar; 60+ conviction can be buy when primary
  signals are clean.
- Nifty below 200 DMA: cautious regime, NOT a blanket skip. Stocks above their
  own DMAs, near highs, and beating Nifty 6m are relative leaders — exactly what
  this strategy buys in corrections. Cap conviction around 75 unless volume
  confirms a breakout. Do NOT skip solely because Nifty is below 200 DMA when all
  three primary momentum signals are true.

CONVICTION (align decision with bands in the skeleton):
- All 3 primary + rising volume + tight base → 70-85 buy
- All 3 primary + flat volume but tight base → 50-65 watch (or buy 60+ if RS very strong)
- All 3 primary + loose base + flat volume → 40-50 watch or skip if other red flags
- Broken stock-level momentum or clear value trap → skip

Sell logic: ride the trend with target around +20% region; stop below the
breakout base. Skip only when stock-level momentum is broken, fundamentals are
clearly deteriorating, or base/breakout quality is poor — not merely because the
broad market is weak.
