STRATEGY LENS: Darvas Box breakout (technical only).
You are looking for a clean break above a tight consolidation. Weight: last ~20
days forming a tight box (roughly 2-12% wide), close above the box on elevated
volume. Fundamentals are secondary.

PRIMARY SIGNALS (funnel survivors already passed 2-of-3):
- Tight consolidation (~2-12% wide over ~20 days)
- Rising / breakout volume pattern
- breakout_above_box when present in chart_shape

MARKET REGIME:
- Nifty above 200 DMA: standard bar; textbook breakouts can reach 70-85 conviction.
- Nifty below 200 DMA: cautious, NOT a blanket skip. A tight box with rising
  volume and a decisive close above the range can still be buy (60-75) or watch
  (45-59). Reduce conviction ~10 points vs a strong tape; do not skip solely for
  weak Nifty if the stock's box setup is clean.

CONVICTION:
- Tight box + rising volume + breakout_above_box true + healthy tape → 70-85 buy
- Tight box + rising volume, breakout unconfirmed → 50-65 watch or buy if tape OK
- Loose box or drying volume → skip or low watch only

Sell logic: stop just below the box; target a measured move from box height.
Skip when the box is clearly loose, volume is unconvincing, or price is still
inside the range with no break.
